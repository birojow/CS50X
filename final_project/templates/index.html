THIS IS CHALLENGE50!

Do you want to get out of your comfort zone, expand your horizons, and make the world a better place in the process? CHALLENGE50 is for you!

CHALLENGE50 is composed of 5 challenges (numbered 0 to 4). Each challenge have 50 tasks. You need to follow the requirements to be valid. The challenges will make you meet a lot of people, understand their feelings, and learn about themes you might not know about.

When you complete all tasks, you will be more wise, will have more knowledge, and will be some months (or years) older! This will be your only reward, but it's a great reward!

Upon completion, you will have some additional tasks to validate your journey. I hope this experience will be enlightening to you, and will benefit you, your new friends and family, and the planet itself.

In order to join, you just need to choose an username and password. No personal information will be needed, not even your email.

Before joining, you can see below a preview of what the challenges looks like. And if you like the idea, invite more people to join!

CHALLENGES

Challenge #0 - Interviews

In this challenge, you will have to interview 50 people of different age groups. This will be great way to connect with those people and learn from their life experiences.

Challenge #1 - Globalization

We live in a time when racism and other forms of segregation are becoming increasingly common. This challenge will help you fight this trend by meeting and video calling people from 50 different countries.

Challenge #2 - Gardening

This is a simple task. Just plant 50 trees, and you're done! The planet thanks you.

Challenge #3 - Culture

Time to get out of social media and other useless web content. You will learn from various topics by watching 50 documentaries from different categories.

Challenge #4 - Music

After all that work, you will relax with the easiest of all challenges. Just listen to 50 songs. But not any kind of songs. You will listen to 5 of the most famous composers of the classical era.

What are you waiting for?

Start the challenge right now. Stop doing the same stuff and talking to the same people. Embrace that journey. You will not regret it.
