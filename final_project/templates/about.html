About CHALLENGE50

This website was created as a final project for the Harvard's CS50x, an introduction to the intellectual enterprises of computer science and the art of programming.

In it's Final Project page, the instructors make that request: "All that we ask is that you build something of interest to you, that you solve an actual problem, that you impact your community, or that you change the world. Strive to create something that outlives this course.

I don't know how to change the world. It's to big a task to anyone. In a world with almost 8 billion people, making any sort of impact is something that most of us will live and die without achieving. But I believe that anyone can, and should, make an impact to the people they care about. The people who are close to them.

In order to do that, I decided to challenge people to search for the unknown. To do things that they are not used to. To try to better understand others and learn and admire things outside they preferences, beyond what is popular. I'm sure that anyone who complete CHALLENGE50 will never forget that experience.

I hope to achieve more than just a certificate. I hope this idea outlives myself in your memories.
