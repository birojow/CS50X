CHALLENGE #0

Interviews

  • GOAL: Interview 50 people from different age groups.
  • REQUIREMENTS:
    • The interviewed should be from 5 different age groups:
      • 10 people from 16 to 25 years old;
      • 10 people from 26 to 35 years old;
      • 10 people from 36 to 45 years old;
      • 10 people from 46 to 55 years old;
      • 10 people from 56 to 65 years old;
    • All interviews should be made in person, or via video call. No phone calls, instant messages or emails;
    • You should include these questions in all interviews:
      • What was the best moment of your life?
      • If you could go back in time, what advice would you give yourself?
      • What would you not change in your life under any circumstances?
      • If you could go anywhere and do anything, what would you do right now?
    • Record or take notes of all answers, compare them and see the differences and similarities;
  • SUGGESTIONS:
    • Combine this challenge with Challenge #1;
    • If you want to make it harder, do not repeat ages. You should interview exactly one person of each age, from 16 to 65;
    • Add more questions;
    • Be sure to inform the interviewed that you will record/take notes of the answers, but will not reveal the identities or any detail that could identify them.
